The 1975 (self-titled)

In their first self titled album, the 1975 aimed to have album artwork that was both “chic and simple.” Inspired by the Chanel logo, the 1975 created a brand off of a simple neon rectangle in black and white. Their almost 80s vibe also appeared in the rest of the album’s artwork, with photos taken for each song by George Daniel, a member of the band. “We wanted something to look like a brand than a band,” Healy said.
