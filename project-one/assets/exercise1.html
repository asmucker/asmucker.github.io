Red Rose / Regular

Red Rose was designed by Jaikishan Patel and released in 2018 by his independently owned and operated company, MagicType, in Mumbai, India ( Red Rose). Red Rose is an exclusively designed Latin typeface, inspired by the Serifs typeface. Its distinguishing factors are its triangular-shaped serif design and curved strokes near the end of the axes. I was drawn to Red Rose because of its unique flared character strokes and medium contrast in stroke weight.
